Frequently Asked Questions

What is PMS, and how can Cycl. help?

PMS (Pre-Menstrual Syndrome) is when a person has emotional and physical symptoms that happen before or during their period. These symptoms may differ per person, but can include moodiness, sadness, anxiety, breast tenderness, bloating, cramping, and acne. The symptoms generally go away after the first few days of a period.

Cycl. has been made from a traditional blend of active ingredients known for their benefits in pain relief, stress relief, hormonal balance and calming effects. For more information, see product ingredients page here.

How is Cycl. different from over-the-counter pain relief medications?

Unlike other methods of PMS relief that target painful PMS symptoms in real-time, Cycl. is a preventative option that prepares your body daily to tackle PMS symptoms so they are less painful and more manageable.

Cycl, is an easily digestible, strawberry flavored gummy, derived from proven active ingredients such as Chasteberry Plant Extract, Lemon Balm, Vitamin B6 and Dong Quai.

See ingredients here for more info.

How long will one Cycl. PMS Relief bottle last me?

Every menstruation cycle is different, but studies show that a cycle typically lasts between 28 to 30 days on average. With this in mind, Cycl. bottles contain 60 gummies, intended to last you through a full 30 day cycle, if used only as directed per our full product details here.

Who should take Cycl. gummies?

Any person of menstruating age can take Cycl. to assist with their PMS symptoms.

For best results, take two Cycl. gummies daily with or without food. Please consult your doctor if you are pregnant, breastfeeding, or planning to get pregnant before taking Cycl.
