It's A
Family Affair

Like countless others, my mom, sister, and I struggled with monthly PMS symptoms: mood swings sending my dad for cover, bloating, cravings, acne and low energy. As a 15 year old athlete, my PMS symptoms often had me sidelined from an earlier age – and not able to participate in the activities I loved.

Hi, I'm Zoe.

In our family, it's all about girl power. With three girls, including two of menstruating age, and my mom, there's often a whirlwind of PMS (Pre-Menstrual Syndrome) swirling around.

Frustrated by the lack of relief out there, my mom and I, with a little help from dad, developed Cycl.

Cycl. is a PMS symptom relief gummy designed to support people with periods at every stage of their cycle. Made from a proven blend of active ingredients renowned for their pain relief, stress reduction, hormonal balance, and overall calmness* – all in delicious, strawberry flavoured, easy-to-digest gummies.

For me, Cycl. had to meet several criteria

  1. It had to be effective. It is personally tested.
  2. The gummies had to taste divine, hence the strawberry flavor.
  3. It had to be easy to digest – simply take two gummies daily, every day of the month, to prepare your body for an easy period.
  4. Affordable, at $24.99.
  5. It had to serve a meaningful purpose, contributing profits to a cause close to our family’s heart. Cycl. is proud to dedicate 50% of its profits to SafiCycle, powered by LifeStraw.

Cycl. has been a game changer for the women in my family.
Join the Cycl. family. Try Cycl. for yourself, while helping to contribute to fighting period poverty in Kenya.
